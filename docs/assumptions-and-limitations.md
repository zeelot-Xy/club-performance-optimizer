# Assumptions and Limitations

## Assumptions
- The club has at most 25 active players
- Weekly data is available before each match
- The Coach/Admin enters or verifies weekly indicators
- Synthetic attributes reflect realistic football contexts

## Limitations
- The system is not a replacement for full tactical staff analysis
- Recommendation quality depends on data quality
- The ML component is optional and supplementary
- The system focuses on one club and does not generalize across league-wide operations
- Recommendations are decision support, not automated decisions
