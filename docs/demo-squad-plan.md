# Demo Squad Plan

## Overview
The demo squad should remain realistic, balanced, and small enough for a final-year project. The target squad size is 20 to 25 players.

## Recommended Squad Breakdown
- 2 goalkeepers
- 7 defenders
- 6 midfielders
- 5 forwards
- 0 to 5 flexible extra players depending on final total

## Positional Balance Principles
- Include left-sided and right-sided players where possible.
- Avoid a squad that is overloaded with only central players.
- Ensure enough defensive and midfield depth to support all approved formations.
- Avoid unrealistic imbalance such as too many strikers and too few defenders.

## Formation Support Requirement
The final demo squad must be capable of supporting:
- `4-3-3`
- `4-4-2`
- `4-2-3-1`
- `3-5-2`

## Practical Composition Guidance
- Goalkeepers should be clearly identified and limited to a small realistic count.
- Defenders should include center-backs and full-backs or wing-backs.
- Midfielders should include defensive, central, and attacking roles where possible.
- Forwards should include central and wide attacking options.
- Flexible players may be used to improve tactical coverage across multiple formations.

## Academic Rationale
A balanced squad design improves the realism of recommendation testing and prevents the system from being biased by an unrealistic player distribution.
